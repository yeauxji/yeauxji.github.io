Bio

My name is Yu Ji (季雨). I obtained my PhD degree from University of Delaware (UDel), advised by Jingyi Yu. Before that, I received my MS from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore and BE from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), China.

My research interests include computer vision, computational photography, computer graphics and machine learning, in particular, dynamic shape reconstruction, computational imaging and lighting, and mobile computational photography applications.

I teach courses at UDel in Fall 2021 & Spring 2022.
